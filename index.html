What happens when you change the way you look at things?

That's the central question behind reVISION.

The concept is built on a profound, powerful truth: when you change the way you see things, the things you see change. It's the path where the unwanted, the discarded, the unvalued, or the simply ignored meet our attention, ingenuity, and resourcefulness, revealing a world of astonishing potential in unexpected places.

reVISION inspires us to look with curiosity and wonder, not just at the places and things around us, but also within ourselves. This journey reveals how a shift in perspective can lead to brilliant creations, remarkable solutions, and extraordinary innovations, while also helping us discover the simple, unseen wonders and profound insights in our everyday lives.

From discarded materials → valuable art and innovation. From overlooked spaces → vibrant communities. From scientific accident → world-changing discovery. From personal setback → unexpected superpower. And all those → yet to be revealed.

Your unique lens can illuminate powerful stories of reVISION.

The reVISION Awards

An inaugural short film competition celebrating stories that reveal the extraordinary potential that exists in surprising places.

We Invite You to Submit

  • 3–10 minute short films
  • Documentary, narrative, or experimental formats
  • Original work — new or previously completed

Judging Criteria

Films will be judged on storytelling craft, cinematic quality, and emotional impact. Bonus consideration given for demonstrating Exceptional Ingenuity in the filmmaking process, including creative use of constraints, innovative narrative style and perspective, and resourcefully blending equipment and materials.
